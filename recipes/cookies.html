Blueberry White Cholocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A soft and sweet treat that is easy to make and even easier to eat! Simple steps and a total baking time of less than an hour make this a perfect evening dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flower
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened
  • 1/3 + 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/3 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup white Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Thaw blueberries (microwave or stovetop) and let cool.
  2. Combine butter and sugar in a mixer and beat until creamy/fluffy
  3. Mash your blueberries and add them to your mixer. Beat on high so the blueberries get basically pureed into the mixture.
  4. Gradually fold in flower, baking powder, and salt until a dough forms. Add the chocolate chips.
  5. PREHEAT oven to 400 degrees F and place dough in the freezer for 30 minutes.
  6. Shape dough into 12 balls and bake for 10 to 13 minutes or until edges brown.
  7. Let cool 5 to 10 minutes.
