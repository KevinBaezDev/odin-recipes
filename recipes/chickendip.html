Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

A creamy and cheesy blend of chicken, hot sauce, and ranch. The recipe leaves you with a perfectly baked chicken, shredded and ready for dipping. Pair with your favorite chips and you've got a snack full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot Original Cayanne Pepper Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles

Steps

  1. PREHEAT oven to 350 degrees F. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Spoon into shallow 1-quart baking dish.
  2. BAKE 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through; stir. Sprinkle with green onions, if desired, and serve with chips, crackers and/or cut up veggies.
